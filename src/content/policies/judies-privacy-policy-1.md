# Privacy Policy

Application "Judies"

## Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28th, 2017

Opendream Co., Ltd. (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates application “Judies”. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the application.

We use your Personal Information only for providing and improving the Site. By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### Collection and Use of Information

We collect personal information from our users so we could create their accounts, operate the services we provide, improve the services we provide and to be able to contact our users in case we need to. We store the collected information on PlayFab servers.

### Log in with Facebook Connect

You have the option to log in into Judies using Facebook Connect. This sign-in service will authenticate your identity and give you the option to share some personal information.  
Such sign-in services allow the collection and storage of user information and we may do this, within the boundaries of their terms and policies. Some of the information collected may include, but is not limited to: your name, username, sign-in service ID number, email address, and profile picture

### Collecting Data as You Play

As you play Judeis, we monitor how you interact with the game and with other players. We may collect and store this information. Any such information is stored by PlayFab.

### Facebook Analytics, PlayFab and Unity Analytics

We use the services of Facebook Analytics, PlayFab and Unity. All three services collect user information on different levels. The information collected might be both technical and/or behavioural. This Privacy Policy does not cover the way these companies store and use the acquired information, nor we have control over this. Please refer to the Privacy Policy of the respective service provider to examine them in detail:

-   [https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy/](https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy/)
-   [https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/](https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/)
-   [https://playfab.com/privacy/](https://playfab.com/privacy/)

### Advertising Networks

It is possible that we display advertisements while you play Judeis. The Ad Network that we partner with, Unity Ads, may collect some behavioural information for you and share it with the advertisers. To find out how Unity handle private information, please, refer to their Privacy Policy – [https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy)

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
