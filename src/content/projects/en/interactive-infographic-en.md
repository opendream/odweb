# สร้างเสริมสุขภาพดีทั้ง 3 อ.

An innovative way of disseminating health knowledge regarding nutrition, exercise and emotion management through interactive infographic in which users are able to choose information that is most suitable for them.

1.  Nutrition Application – “Snap before you eat” ([iOS](https://itunes.apple.com/th/app/%E0%B9%81%E0%B8%8A%E0%B8%B0%E0%B8%81-%E0%B8%AD%E0%B8%99%E0%B8%AB%E0%B8%A1-%E0%B8%B3/id989677315?mt=8)/[Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opendream.doctor.shootmyfood))
2.  Exercise Application – “Measure your strength” ([iOS](https://itunes.apple.com/th/app/wad-kalang/id1045353948?ls=1&mt=8)/[Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opendream.doctor.exerciseapp))
3.  Emotional management Application – “ Surviving stress” ([iOS](https://itunes.apple.com/th/app/kheriyd-xyu-di/id1044616712?ls=1&mt=8)/[Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opendream.moodapp))
