# PUNJAI

Punjai is a second hand goods platform that connects owners of goods, buyers and social projects. The income generated by the sales would be donated to social projects of the owner’s choice. Owners and buyers can easily connect and communicate through the Punjai platform as the system provides all-round technical support in terms of product posting, chat box, as well as online payment.
