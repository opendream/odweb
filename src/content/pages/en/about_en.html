About Us

We work on social issues that we aim to set the foundation and examples to lead sustainable changes.

What do we do?

Opendream utilizes its expertise in digital technology to innovate and develop tools targeted at areas of health, education and livelihood. We work specifically on issues that we aim to set the foundation and examples to lead sustainable changes, for example,

Develop investigation game to promote awareness on corruption among teenagers

Create digital tools that support self-care

Set up disease surveillance system for remote areas

Create fund raising system that allows donors to donate specifically to projects of interest

Produce game to educate teenagers on safe sex practices

Teach grade 4 students to send balloons to the edge of space

We believe changes to the society would not able to be achieved through the work of one person or one organization. Therefore, we actively collaborate with other organizations both in Thailand and abroad, including Google, Oxfam GB, Skoll Global Threats Fund, Chiang Mai University and so on and leverage on the synergy to drive sustainable impacts.
