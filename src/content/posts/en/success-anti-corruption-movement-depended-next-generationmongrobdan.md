Opendream team was interviewed in the Mong Rob Dann program, Channel 3 Family on 27th February, 2017. Program was available for download online from 1st March, 2017 onward.

Opendream believes that game is an effective media for education and therefore developed the game Corrupt with the aim to raise public awareness on corruption in the sense that corruption is not only the problem of the people in power or those who are engaged in dishonest and fraudulent activities. Instead, it’s a societal problem and everyone should have a part to play in order to solve it.
