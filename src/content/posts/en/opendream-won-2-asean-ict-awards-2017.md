Opendream was selected to be one of the representatives of Thailand by the Ministry of Digital Economy and Society (DE) and Digital Economy Promotion Agency (DEPA) to present the game Corrupt and Participatory One-health Disease Detection system (PODD) in the final round of the ASEAN ICT Awards 2017. The finalists were selected among hundreds of projects sent in from 11 ASEAN countries.

The final round was held on November 29-30, 2017 in Cambodia.

The anti-corruption game **Corrupt** won Silver Award in the category of Digital Content  
**Participatory One-health Disease Detection system (PODD)** won Bronze Award in the category of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).
